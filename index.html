About

I'm Jonathan Mah, a second-year student at the University of Washington studying Applied and Computational Mathematical Sciences, Biochemistry, and Microbiology. This is my personal website, and I hope to keep it updated with ongoing projects as well as notes from my studies.

Research

I'm currently engaged with undergraduate research at the Bloom Lab, in the Basic Sciences Division of the Fred Hutchinson Cancer Research Center. I'm working with graduate mentor, Sarah Hilton, on identifying sites positive selection in the Influenza virus surface protein Hemagglutinin (HA)
